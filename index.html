01 · How It Works

From New Lead to Logged Outcome

Every lead — whether it comes from the intake form or a Google Sheet — flows through the same simple, automatic path.

1
Lead Arrives

A new lead is captured

Leads come in from the patient intake form or a connected Google Sheet. Each one is added to the CRM automatically — no manual entry by staff.

2
Within 2 Minutes

Sarah places the first call

The patient is called within 2 minutes of submitting the form or being added to the Google Sheet — using a Twilio phone number and a Vapi voice agent.

3
On The Call

She offers a clear next step

Sarah asks whether to transfer the call to your team now, or send a message so the patient can book their physical therapy appointment.

4
Logged & Messaged

Outcome saved, message sent

The patient's choice is written back to the CRM instantly, and a booking message is sent immediately after the call ends.

02 · On The Call

Every Outcome, Handled Automatically

When Sarah reaches a patient, she offers to transfer the call or send a booking message. Based on what the patient says, one of these outcomes is recorded.

📞

Transfer to a human

If the patient wants to speak with someone now, Sarah transfers the live call straight to your team.

→ +1 949 276 5401
💬

Send a booking message

The patient gets a text immediately after the call so they can complete their physical therapy booking on their own time.

SMS from +1 888 914 2182
⏱️

Call back in 5 minutes

If now isn't a good time, the patient can ask Sarah to try again — and a fresh callback is placed in 5 minutes.

📅

Schedule for another day

The patient can ask to be called on a specific day, and Sarah will call then — confirmed against the clinic's schedule.

🚫

Do not call again

If the patient says not to call again, they're marked accordingly and Sarah will not contact them again.

⚠️

Wrong number

If it turns out to be the wrong number, the lead is flagged and no further calls are made.

↳
Every outcome syncs to the CRM. Whatever the patient chooses — transfer, message, callback, scheduled day, opt-out, or wrong number — updates their record the moment the call ends.
