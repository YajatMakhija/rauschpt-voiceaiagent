01 · Process Flow

Every Lead, Logged in Four Steps

Each lead — submitted through the intake form or added to the Google Sheet — moves through the same four steps automatically, with no manual handoff.

1
Step 1 · Intake

Lead is received from the form or Google Sheet

A new lead enters the system through one of two sources: the patient intake form (n8n) or the connected Google Sheet. The record is created in the CRM automatically — no manual entry by staff is required.

Open intake form ↗Open Google Sheet ↗
2
Step 2 · First Call

Sarah places the first call within 2 minutes

Within two minutes of the lead being received, the agent dials the patient using the configured Twilio number, routed through a Vapi voice agent.

3
Step 3 · Conversation

The patient is offered a next step

The agent asks whether to transfer the call to a team member immediately, or to send a message the patient can use to complete their physical therapy booking on their own time.

4
Step 4 · Logging

Outcome is written and message is sent

The patient's response is recorded against their CRM record as soon as the call ends, and the booking message is sent immediately afterward. The result appears live on the CRM board.

Open CRM dashboard ↗
02 · Call Outcomes

Six Outcomes, Handled Live on the Call

The agent is configured to recognise six distinct patient responses. Whichever one occurs, the result is written to the patient's CRM record the moment the call ends.

📞

Transfer the call

If the patient asks to speak with someone, the live call is transferred to the clinic's team line.

→ +1 949 276 5401
💬

Send a booking message

Once the call ends, a text message is sent to the patient containing the link they need to complete their physical therapy booking.

SMS from +1 888 914 2182
⏱️

Callback in 5 minutes

If the patient asks to be called back in five minutes, a new call attempt is scheduled and placed automatically at that time.

📅

Callback on a specific day

If the patient asks to be called on a particular day, the agent will place the call on that day, subject to confirmation against the clinic schedule.

🚫

Do not call again

If the patient asks not to be contacted again, the record is marked accordingly and no further calls are placed to that number.

⚠️

Wrong number

If the patient indicates this is not their number, the lead is flagged in the CRM and removed from the active calling list.

↳
All outcomes are written back to the CRM. Regardless of which outcome occurs — transfer, message, callback, scheduled day, opt-out, or wrong number — the patient's record is updated as soon as the call ends.
