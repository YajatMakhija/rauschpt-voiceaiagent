01 · Process Flow

Every Lead, Logged in Four Steps

Each lead — submitted through the intake form or added to the Google Sheet — moves through the same four steps automatically, with no manual handoff.

1
Step 1 · Intake

Lead enters the CRM

A patient is added to the Google Sheet and the lead is created in the CRM automatically.

Open Google Sheet ↗
2
Step 2 · First Call

Patient is called within 2 minutes

The voice agent calls the patient from +1 213 758 1768 within 2 minutes via Vapi. Off-hour leads wait in the queue.

3
Step 3 · Two Options

SMS or call transfer offered

After confirming interest, the patient is offered two options: receive a booking SMS, or be transferred live to the clinic team at +1 949 276 5401.

4
Step 4 · CRM Sync

SMS sent or call transferred

If the patient picks the SMS, it goes out immediately from +1 888 914 2182. If they pick transfer, the call is routed live to +1 949 276 5401. Either way, the outcome is written to the CRM.

Open CRM dashboard ↗
🕘Operating hours (Pacific Time) — leads received outside these hours wait in the queue:
  • Monday – Friday   7:00 AM – 7:00 PM
  • Saturday   7:00 AM – 2:00 PM
  • Sunday   Closed
  • Closed holidays   Memorial Day · Thanksgiving · Christmas · New Year's Day
  • Early close at 2:00 PM   Christmas Eve & New Year's Eve
🎯Six different situations can be handled within a single call.
  • Transfer the call to the clinic team line (+1 949 276 5401)
  • Send a booking SMS to the patient (from +1 888 914 2182)
  • Schedule a callback in 5 minutes if the patient is unavailable
  • Schedule a callback for a specific day at the patient's request
  • Mark as do-not-contact if the patient declines further calls
  • Flag as wrong number and remove from the active calling list
